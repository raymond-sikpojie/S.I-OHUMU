About me

S.I Ohumu is a storyteller and community organizer engaging Nigeria’s youth through art, for climate action, mental wellness, and gender equity. She was most recently an assistant producer on the BBC documentary ‘Sex for Grades’ which has led to the reintroduction of the anti sexual harassment bill in the Nigerian senate. S.I is also founder of Ubini.org, collaborating partner of the International Association of Psychiatric Nurses, and co founder of #SpaceBenin, a youth community promoting self expression, mental wellness, building meaningful friendships and more. She currently hosts #ClimateActionMinute, is a member of the National Youth Climate Innovation Hub, has had her writing exhibited at the Venice Biennale, and has worked for the BBC, British Council, Edo State Government, Silverbird Communications, among others. You can find her across media platforms (@si_ohumu), producing social impact stories using film, text and sound, laughing, managing a community, and climbing trees.
